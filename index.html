THE HOMEPAGE OF THE BEST DAMN TUK-TUK DRIVER IN THE LAND

Mr. Ramorn

Mr. Ramorn is a Tuk-Tuk driver servicing Siem Reap, Cambodia, home to the ancient Angkor temple complexes of the Khmer Empire (802 - 1431A.D.), and specializes in giving Tuk-Tuk tours of these magnificient and spectacular structures. He is employed by, and operates out of, The Siem Reap Hostel, where I was staying on my visit to the Angkor temples back in December of 2015. I am speaking from personal experience, that both the hostel, and Mr. Ramorn, are absolutely awesome and that will you not find a better accommodation or Tuk-Tuk driver when planning a trip to Siem Reap.
